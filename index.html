What is GravySlam?

GravySlam is more than just wrestling — it's a 250-year-old tradition steeped in rich, meaty history. Originating in a secret turnip barn in rural Wigan in 1775, GravySlam began as a series of spirited disputes over who made the best stew. Over the centuries, it evolved into the spectacle you see today: elbow drops, snack belts, pug security forces, and legendary family rivalries.

The Gravy Code, passed down through generations, dictates honourable use of condiments, stylish entrance music, and the sacred “No Slip Zone” around custard tart displays. Today, GravySlam hosts sell-out events across village halls and sports clubs, with fans of all ages pledging allegiance to their chosen fighters. Percy Security ensures peace. Pudding ensures naps. And the chaos? That’s just tradition.
